SUMMARY

Our construction company has been founded 10 years ago, at the very peak of the building frenzy in the US…

Azadi Consulting (PTY) LTD was founded in 2017 as legal entity to conduct business by MR N Mthembu taking his rich extensive experience in the built-in environment and engineering stretching over a decay (12 Years). MR. N Mthembu is a registered Professional Quantity Surveyor (Pr.4786) with south Africa council of Quantity Surveyor Profession.

Azadi Consulting is Multi-disciplinary Organisation providing Service of Quantity Surveying, Project Management, Property Services and Capacity Building and the organisation is registered with relevant professional boards.


Vission

Our vision is to attain sustainable growth of our portfolio to become the most sought after and distinguished Multi-disciplinary firm in South Africa..


Mission

Our company motto ‘behind figures’ is based on the following key values which we all share, and which work synergistically to form the solid foundation upon which Azadi Consulting has been built and continues to develop and grow: Integrity, Quality, Experience, Versatility and Resilience.
We believe strongly in driving a culture of excellence, with stringent attention to detail and quality, as well as offering a consistently superior service to our clients. Our people have been carefully selected and are well placed to provide advice and a professional service.
